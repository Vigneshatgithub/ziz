How we do


We are at level zero. We have named providing education to kids as "level zero" :)

Out there, are enormous number of helpless kids in need of education .

Nothing comes or goes free. Even "giving away or charity" comes at the cost of happiness

We took initiative on July 2009 to collect minimum amount of Rs. 50 or 100 monthly from circle of healthy friends we always have.

We will definitely appreciate your contribution of money. You can also start contributing by spreading the word about Ziz.

Our Contributions

Monthly


  • Paying school fees for 3-4 kids on monthly basis. ( Gangadharaa Matric school, Navalpur, Ranipet, Vellore, Tamilnadu)

  • Donating money to an aided school that offers free education to kids. ( Mangalaamabaal school, Vellore, Tamilnadu )

Occasional


  • Assisted 2 college students by providing money for exam fees.

  • Donated some money for a kid who is struggling for life near karur.

  • Provided money to a school to buy fans for the classrooms.
